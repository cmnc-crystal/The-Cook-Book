Jogaetang

Ingredients

  • 500g medium-sized clams ,rinsed
  • 1 tbsp gochujang chilli paste or white miso
  • 2 large garlic cloves , finely chopped
  • 3 spring onions , whites finely sliced, greens roughly chopped
  • 2 handfuls beansprouts
  • 1 green chilli , cut into matchsticks
  • toasted sesame oil , to serve
  • cooked rice , to serve
  • kimchi or pickled cucumber, to serve

How to Make this Clam Stew - Jogaetang

  1. Drain the rinsed clams well and place them in a saucepan (with a lid) that fits them in a single layer.
  2. Pour over cold water to just cover (about 750ml should do it), then stir in the chilli paste or
    white miso, the garlic and the spring onion whites.
  3. Cover with a lid, bring to the boil, then turn down the heat and simmer gently for 2-3 mins until
    the clams have all opened.
  4. Turn off the heat and stir through the beansprouts and chilli.
  5. Season with salt to taste, and decant into one large or two smaller bowls.
  6. Top with the spring onion greens and a drizzle of sesame oil, and enjoy with rice and
    something sharp like kimchi or pickled cucumber.
