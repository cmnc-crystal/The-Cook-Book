Beef Pho

Ingredients

  • Marrow bones and Meaty bones
  • Thinly sliced raw meat
  • Yellow onions
  • Whole garlic
  • Fresh ginger
  • Whole spices: star anise, cloves, cinnamon sticks, black cardamom pods, fennel seeds, coriander seeds
  • Salt
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar
  • Rice noodles

How to Make this Beef Pho

  1. Quick boil – Remove impurities from beef with a 5 minute boil, it’s the path to a beautiful clear soup.
  2. Wash the bones to get all the icky scum off.
  3. Simmer for 3 hours – bones, beef, water, onion, ginger and spices (cinnamon, cardamom, coriander, star anise).
  4. Remove brisket – some is used for Pho topping.
  5. Simmer 40 minutes further with just bones.
  6. Then, strain.
  7. Ladle into bowls over noodles and pile on toppings.
