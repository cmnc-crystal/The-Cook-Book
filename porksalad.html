Pork Salad

Ingredients

  • 500g pork tenderloin , trimmed of fat
  • vegetable oil , for brushing
  • Â¼ white cabbage , shredded
  • 1 cucumber , cut into matchsticks
  • 5 celery stalks, cut into matchsticks
  • 3 spring onions , finely sliced
  • 1 red chilli , seeded and finely chopped
  • 2 trimmed stems of lemongrass , finely sliced
  • zest of 1 lime
  • a handful each of coriander and mint leaves, chopped

For the Dressing

  • 1 tsp golden caster sugar
  • juice of 2 limes
  • 1 fresh red chilli , deseeded and finely chopped
  • a handful of coriander stems or roots, chopped
  • 1 tbsp each sesame oil, Thai fish sauce and light soy sauce
  • 50g sesame seeds , dry roasted until golden

How to Make this Vietnamese Pork Salad

  1. The day before: make the dressing. Put the sugar and lime juice in a pan with 1 tbsp water and
    bring to the boil to dissolve the sugar. Add the chilli and coriander and stir well,
    then pulse in a blender until smooth.
  2. Tip into a bowl, then stir in the sesame oil, fish sauce, soy sauce and sesame seeds to make a dressing.
    Cover and chill until needed.
  3. Two hours before serving: heat a griddle pan. Preheat the oven to 200C/gas 6/fan 180C.
    Brush the pork with oil and griddle on all sides for a few minutes until seared.
  4. Transfer to a baking tray and put in the oven for about 10-12 minutes until cooked through.
  5. Cool, thinly slice against the grain of the meat. Tip into a bowl and pour over half the dressing.
  6. To serve: toss the remaining salad ingredients in a bowl with the remaining dressing.
    Pile on to a platter, top with the pork slices and spoon over any juices.
